About Meow Cats

Passion in knowing and sharing knowledge about cats drove this website to be created. MeowCats is a free website which aims to provide entertainment by showcasing cats' different personalities. This also highlights how funny cats are and how adorable they are in their own ways which can take away negativities in human beings. Moreover, this website wants to spread awareness that cats have their own feelings too. Like humans, they need someone who cares about them and loves them unconditionally. Hence, acts of cruelty against animals in general are not acceptable that is why awareness is important.
